Out Of Controll

Out Of Controll is a versus arcade racing game, where you use any tools at your disposal to win a race

Shoot carrot bombs, lay melon mines, throw eggs at others' windshields, and try to not fall off the track.
This was my first big Unreal project, so I worked mostly in Blueprints.

I was responsible for the car physics and tooling for importing car models into Unreal.

Most of the pre-made configurable cars in Unreal have realistic physics, that don't align with our more wacky, kart-racer type vision that we had for the game.
This meant that I had to implement the physics myself.
